College Fest

The college celebrated yet another glorious year with its annual cultural festival. It marks the team spirit, passion, exploration beyond the walls of classrooms and nearly hundred years of the grandeur of the institution. The unmatched spirit of students welcomed the pleasant air of Spring and adorned the campus with colours and joviality. The ECA Societies of the college astounded everyone with their diverse and innovative ideas and sincere hard work in hosting competitions which witnessed significant footfall from colleges in and around Delhi. The competitions offered a platform for showcasing the remarkable talent of students and prized the beauty of various art forms.

Day 2 began with the group dance competition by Dance Society and A Capella Competition by Music Society. The afternoon saw some great performances at Bilingual Slam Poetry Competition, Declamation Competition Debating Society and Solo Singing Competition by Music society. The musical evening commenced with the Delhi-based Rock band who took the crowd by a storm. However, the star attraction was the much-awaited Hindustani Music Producer and singer, whose stimulating beats mesmerized the crowds and ended the festivities on a gratifying note.

The euphoric success of the festival was the fruition of sincere hard work, diligence and determination put in by the Students Union of the college who have set an example for the several celebrations in the futurity.
