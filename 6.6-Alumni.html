Alumni

Eduka is the cradle of leadership and well recognized for nurturing talent. Since its inception, it has carved a unique niche for itself. It is the alma mater of a phenomenally large number of eminent people who have not just excelled themselves in diverse professions but reached the pinnacle of success.


The official Eduka Alumni Portal is an online social community for the alumni of Eduka University launched in line with its vision to be a College of Global Choice.
Alumni are the brand ambassadors of an institution, personifying its ethos. Their experience, learning and contributions to the society bring prestige to their alma mater.
The illustrious history of Eduka University is defined by its cherished alumni, who have contributed immensely for the growth and development of their beloved alma mater. For the College, its alumni are always amongst the most important stakeholders. As such, the Official Alumni Portal was launched with the aim to enhance alumni engagement, to foster a greater sense of a community belongingness and to deepen institutional ties with the alumni. Through this portal, the College and the alumni will be able to engage in a mutually beneficial and meaningful long term communication. This portal also is envisaged as a forum for connecting generations of Eduka alumni with each other. The Eduka Alumni Portal personifies the belief of the College to integrate its alumni in the qualitative framework of the institution. The portal will serve as an important channel to further the Alumni-Academia interface of Eduka University.The portal is designed keeping in mind the needs of Alumni. Eduka Alumni, registered as users, can explore a host of features of the portal embedded to facilitate two-way communication between the College and the Alumni. Alumni can also engage directly and contribute to the initiatives of the College. The portal also facilitates alumni to communicate and collaborate with each other.
