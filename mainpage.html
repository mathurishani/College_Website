Principal's Desk

Welcome to the College. You have just embarked on a journey of discovery and revelation, a journey that is as much about you as it is about the larger world that you inhabit. Here in college, embraced by its natural beauty, you will find the space where you can explore, express, and realize yourself. The College with its infrastructure and facilities is at your disposal to help you grow and develop, to enable yourselves and become independent and confident young person who can take on the world on their terms. Its accomplished faculty and friendly support staff will encourage you to claim the College as your special world, one that you will treasure and carry with you always. I welcome the new batch of students and invite them to embark on an intellectually invigorating journey with us.

Our Facilities

Our university serves many facilities which can only be served to you once you visit us!
Till then have a look at few of them below.

World Class Library

The university has a spacious Library that strives to create a lively and enriching intellectual environment for students, faculty, and staff members. It has a lift for PWD access.

Largest Playground

The university has the largest playground in the area which is has temperature 5 degrees less than the outer area surrounded by university.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The food in canteen as well as mess is made with proper sanitation and hygiene. Every 6 months, the cleanliness is checked by higher authorities.
