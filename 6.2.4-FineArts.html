The Fine Arts Society

Fine Arts is a community of art enthusiasts with a motive to provide each other a safe space to stay connected and listen to their art. Here, people not only get a platform to perform their art freely but also get insights into different art styles by interacting with fellow artists. EVENTS Skill Enhancement Workshops: With the motive to help our members get acquainted with various art forms, we organize workshops for different art styles throughout the year by several renowned artists. Social Media Exhibit: The society exhibits artworks of the members on its social media platforms from time to time.

Newsletter: Fine Arts has begun with its own newsletter from this session that includes latest works of the members, updates about recent society events and many other interesting sections. ArtSlam: An annual event organized by Fine Arts where several Fine Arts societies from Delhi University and outside compete in an exhibition competition.
