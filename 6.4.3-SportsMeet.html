Sports Meet

The Annual Sports Festival is one of the grandest celebrations of our college. It is a massive three-day event along with many pre-sports day events in which we organize various inter-college events for both, men and women such as Cross Country Run, and other athletic events for Persons With Disabilities also. Additionally, we conduct numerous events for teaching and non-teaching staff in which they participate competitively with true sportsmanship.
