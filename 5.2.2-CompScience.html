Computer Science Department

About

The College is one of the few select colleges in the University with hi-tech infrastructure, an updated curriculum and excellent faculty to offer 6-semester B.Sc.(H) Computer Science programme. Uniquely, this Course is open to students from all academic backgrounds and does not require any prerequisite knowledge in Computer Science.
We have a dedicated computer science block, which has 3 computer labs and one lecture room. All the labs have latest computer systems and printers which are all connected through LAN. These systems are updated/ replaced from time to time to keep up with the latest technology. Each of the labs and lecture rooms has an overhead projector to enhance the teaching and learning process.
The course covers all aspects, right from the fundamentals such as Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, Databases, Computer Networks to advanced courses such as Microprocessors, Network Programming, Graphics, Advanced Algorithms and so on. This way the course prepares anyone for higher education. There are many further options available for the students after completing this course. They take up the placements offered to them through campus placements. They enroll for M.Sc.(CS)/MCA, M.Sc.(OR), M.Sc.(Informatics) courses after completing B.Sc.(H) Computer Science. In M.Sc. Computer Sc. course of University of Delhi, about 50% of the seats are filled on the basis of merit in B.Sc. (H) Computer Science Examination of University of Delhi. Many of our students also enroll for MBA course after graduating from this course.
Departmental faculty members have interests in varied research fields ranging from Theoretical Computer Science, Artificial intelligence, Cloud Computing, Data Mining, Pattern Recognition and Classification, Software Engineering etc. Research at the undergraduate level is promoted by holding departmental research seminars in each semester. Students are provided exposure to the current technology available in the market through monthly seminars where speakers from the industry are invited.
