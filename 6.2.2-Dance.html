The Dance Society

The Dance Society is known for its uniqueness. The society provides an amazing platform to all the budding dancers and helps them grow in various dance styles and as a whole. The Aim of the society is to represent our college at different levels, national as well as international, and give out as much as possible from our end. As an active society of Eduka they tend to make the college proud by doing the best possible thing from their side.
Eduka Dance Society is confident to fly as high as mighty for excellence of culture of college. Every year, the society organises its festival where a series of competitions and events are conducted.
